# Privacy Policy for Graysta

**Effective Date:** November 1, 2025  
**Last Updated:** November 1, 2025

## Introduction

Graysta ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Graysta mobile application (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Directly

- **Photos and Images:** Images you capture or select for editing within the app
- **User Preferences:** Filter selections and app settings

### Information Collected Automatically

- **Device Information:** Device model, operating system version, and app version
- **Usage Analytics:** App performance data and crash reports (anonymized)
- **Technical Data:** IP address for AI enhancement features (when used)
- **Advertising Data:** Anonymous advertising identifiers for personalized ads (via Google AdMob)

### Permissions We Request

#### Camera Permission

- **Purpose:** To capture new photos for editing
- **Usage:** Only when you choose to take a new photo
- **Storage:** Photos are processed locally and not stored by us

#### Photo Library/Storage Permission

- **Purpose:** To access existing photos and save edited images
- **Usage:** Only when you select photos or save edited images
- **Storage:** You control what images are saved to your device

#### Internet Permission

- **Purpose:** For optional AI enhancement features and advertising
- **Usage:** Only when you choose to use AI enhancement or when ads are displayed
- **Data:** Temporary image processing (not stored) and anonymous advertising data

## How We Use Your Information

### Local Processing

- All basic photo editing and filters are processed locally on your device
- Your photos never leave your device for basic editing functions
- No photos are stored on our servers for offline editing

### AI Enhancement (Optional)

- When you choose AI enhancement, images are temporarily processed by our AI service
- Images are deleted from our servers immediately after processing
- Processing is done securely with encrypted transmission
- You can use the app without AI features - they are completely optional

### App Improvement

- Anonymous usage analytics help us improve app performance
- Crash reports help us identify and fix bugs
- No personal information is included in analytics

### Advertising (Google AdMob)

- We display ads to keep Graysta free for all users
- Ads are served by Google AdMob and may be personalized based on your interests
- We do not share your photos or personal content with advertisers
- Ad preferences can be managed through your device's ad settings
- Rewarded ads unlock premium features temporarily

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage

- Photos and edited images are stored locally on your device
- You have full control over saving and deleting images
- We do not access your photo library without permission

### Data Security

- All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
- We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information
- No payment information is collected (app is free)

### Third-Party Services

- **AI Processing:** Secure cloud processing for AI enhancement features
- **Analytics:** Anonymous usage data for app improvement
- **Google AdMob:** Advertising platform that may collect device identifiers and usage patterns
- **Ad Personalization:** Ads may be tailored based on your interests (can be disabled in device settings)

## Your Rights and Choices

### Photo Control

- You decide which photos to edit and save
- You can delete edited photos at any time from your device
- Original photos remain unchanged unless you choose to overwrite them

### Feature Control

- AI enhancement is completely optional
- You can use all basic features without internet connection
- You can deny camera/photo permissions and still use the app with existing images

### Data Deletion

- Uninstalling the app removes all local data
- Clearing app data removes all preferences and cached images
- No data remains on our servers after AI processing completion

## Children's Privacy

Graysta does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. The app is suitable for all ages when used appropriately. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us.

## International Users

Graysta is designed for global use. If you use the app outside your country of residence, your information may be transferred to and processed in other countries. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by:

- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Providing in-app notifications for significant changes
- Posting updates on our website (if applicable)

- **Photos:** Stored locally on your device, controlled by you
- **AI Processing:** Temporary processing data deleted immediately after completion
- **Analytics:** Anonymous data retained for app improvement purposes only
- **Preferences:** Stored locally until app is uninstalled or data is cleared
